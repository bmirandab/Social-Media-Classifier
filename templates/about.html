About the Project


The purpose of this project is two-fold. The first one is simply Food for Thought. Have you stopped to think how the way we communicate is constantly evolving and changing over time? Around 2.6 billion people around the world use social media, we are able to connect to anyone, anywhere at any time. We have gone from being 'listeners' from the time the radio first broadcasted, to 'watchers' from when television began showing moving pictures in two and three dimensions, to being 'creators' and content generators through social media. We are active participants of media, we have the power to shape what people see and absorb.

The second purpose is to learn. When we think about evolution, we know that to adapt to changes in the environment we must learn and understand. By studying what makes people choose a certain form of communication we can adapt our strategies in life. So, thank you for participating, you have contributed to science, hope you had a good experience!
