Welcome


Welcome to the Social Media Predictor, this is an experiment. You are about to embark on a probability journey to find out if our model can predict your social media platform preferences. Once you finish the survey, you will get a prediction on whether or not you are a user of Facebook, Instagram, Twitter or YouTube.

For this project the data used to train our models came from the PEW Research Center, specifically, from the Core Trends Survey 2018. The survey questions are emulated from the original questionnaire.
